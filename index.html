Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about English Janala

1. How do I create an account?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, English Janala is completely free to use. We believe in making English learning accessible to everyone.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No, you can access most of our lessons without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress and track your learning journey.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Click the "Learn" button above and select a vocabulary category. We offer interactive flashcards, quizzes, and contextual learning exercises.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, we offer digital certificates for completing our advanced level courses. Certificates can be downloaded and shared on professional networks.
